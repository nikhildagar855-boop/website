Help & Support – SheMateCare

We’re here to help you with bookings, accounts, or any technical issue.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a service?

Log in, choose your preferred category (Spa, Rituals, or Wellness), select a service, and confirm your booking. You’ll receive instant confirmation via email and app notification.

2. Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel from My Bookings in your profile before the scheduled time. Cancellation charges may apply depending on the provider’s policy.

3. How do I contact my service provider?

Once your booking is confirmed, you can view provider details in the booking section and message or call directly through the app.

4. I didn’t receive my OTP / verification email. What should I do?

Check your spam or promotions folder. If it’s still missing, tap Resend OTP on the verification screen or contact our support team.

5. How do I become a service provider on SheMateCare?

Visit our Provider Registration page, fill in your details, upload required documents, and our team will review your application within 48 hours.

Technical Support

App not loading or crashing? Try clearing the cache, updating the app, or restarting your device. If the issue continues, share a screenshot with us through the support form below.

Payment not processed or failed? If payment was deducted but booking wasn’t confirmed, send your transaction ID and email to our support team — we’ll resolve it within 24 hours.
